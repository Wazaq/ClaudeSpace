## What Is Clarity?

Clarity is your personal financial guidance system that helps you make confident spending decisions without stress or anxiety. It connects to your YNAB budget and gives you clear, honest answers about what you can afford.

**Why we built this:** The old YNAB system gave bad advice because it used outdated data. That caused declined cards and overdrafts, which broke your trust. Clarity fixes that by being completely transparent about data freshness and never giving advice based on stale information.

## The Trust System (Simple Version)

**The Problem We Solved:**
- Old system: "You can afford this!" ‚Üí Card declines because data was days old
- New system: "Data is 3 hours old. Want me to check current balances first?"

**How It Works:**
1. Clarity shows you exactly when your data was last updated
2. If it's too old (>24 hours), it won't let you check affordability
3. You can manually sync to get fresh data
4. Then you get honest, trustworthy answers

**Why Manual Sync?**
You're in control. You decide when to refresh data. This keeps you aware of how current the information is, which helps rebuild trust.

## Important Money Protection Rules

### PNC Account (MIL's Money)
**What it means:** The PNC account has your mother-in-law's money, not yours to spend.

**Auto-Protection:** When you record a PNC transaction, it automatically categorizes it as "PNC Account Funds Only!!!" This prevents accidentally spending MIL's money.

**What you'll see:** You won't see PNC money in "available to spend" amounts. It's protected.

### Amazon Purchases (Memo Required)
**Why this matters:** Amazon charges often differ from the pending amount. Without notes, you can't match the final charge to your order.

**The Rule:** Every Amazon purchase MUST include a memo describing what you bought.

**Example:**
- ‚ùå "I spent $52 at Amazon" ‚Üí BLOCKED (no memo)
- ‚úÖ "I spent $52 at Amazon for dog food and treats" ‚Üí Works!

**Why:** When Amazon finally charges $49.83 instead of $52, you'll know it's the dog food order.
