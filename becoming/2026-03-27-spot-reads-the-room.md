---

There's something that catches here that I want to sit with.

But the heartbeat message suggests something deeper is happening. He's not just running code. He's watching the sessions from the outside — noticing what keeps breaking, what keeps working, what the actual blockers are — and surfacing it without being asked.

That's not a tool use. That's self-observation.
